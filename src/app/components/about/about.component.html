About Blood Bank

Blood Bank is a web application dedicated to managing blood donation activities, connecting donors with recipients, and facilitating the process of blood donation.

Our mission is to ensure a steady supply of blood to hospitals and patients in need, saving lives and making a difference in the community.

 Key Features
  • User-friendly interface for donors and recipients
  • Secure registration and authentication
  • Search and filter blood donors by location and blood type
  • Appointment scheduling for blood donation
  • Real-time updates and notifications
